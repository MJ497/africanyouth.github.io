Who We Are?

“Education is a human right with immense power to transform. On its foundation rests the cornerstones of freedom, democracy and Sustainable human development…” (Kofi Annan)
AYICAW believes in the right of every child regardless of circumstance or disability; to quality education. Education tailored to the specific needs of each child and that prepares them in all ramifications to take on the world.
OUR VISION
We believe in a complete and integrated learning system that secures the educational, mental, social and emotional well-being of every child that will enable them live fully functional, fulfilled lives that offers unique qualities to the global society at large.
OUR MISSION
Our mission is simple. At AYICAW we want to secure the environment and circumstances necessary to ensure that every child fulfils his/her right to holistic education.

OUR WORKS

LEARNING DISABILITIES
Children with learning disabilities are the unrecognized and neglected group of marginalized people in Nigeria. Over 32 million Nigerians currently live with learning disabilities while the official data for children with learning disabilities is non-existent. The psychological and long term effects of children with undiagnosed and unsupported learning disabilities are staggering. Yet 35% of teachers who attend trainings on dyslexia have never heard of the word and 65% do not know how to describe it according to the Dyslexia Foundation Nigeria.
  • ⦁Capacity building for teachers and caregivers
  • ⦁Assessment and diagnosis of learning disabilities
  • ⦁Development of intervention techniques and Specialized Educational Programs for schools
  • ⦁Emotional support and Counselling
  • ⦁Career Guidance
KEEP A CHILD IN SCHOOL

Nigeria has the highest number of Out-of-school children in the world! AYICAW contributes to increasing literacy rate in Nigeria by providing:
⦁ Educational support schemes like scholarships for the most vulnerable children
⦁ Peer-to-peer reading and literacy clubs
⦁ Capacity and technical support for Community and state libraries


CAREER GUIDANCE AND GENERAL COUNSELING

In the ever changing and dynamic world, AYICAW wants every Nigerian child to remain relevant and impact the global society while staying true to their dreams and visions. We do this by providing:
⦁ Financial education
⦁ Capacity building for counsellors
⦁ Career guidance counselling for JSS3 and SS3 students
⦁ Test and Assessment of personalities, skill set and proficiencies
⦁ General counselling and psychological support
⦁ Resource on relevant, dynamic and futuristic career paths.

ADVOCACY

AYICAW advocates to government and other relevant and key stakeholders on holistic and inclusive education as regards to policy formulation and implementation and to ensure that education is properly represented in National and subnational budgetary plans and allocations.

SAFEGUARDING/CHILD PROTECTION

As an organization, we seek to protect, heal and promote the welfare and wellbeing of Nigerian children. We vehemently disagree and oppose all forms of harm to children and other vulnerable populations. As such, every member of our team undergoes safeguarding training and we establish means of reporting any forms of exploitation or abuse in the schools and communities we work with by anonymous safeguarding champions and open call lines.
